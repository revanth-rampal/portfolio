My Research

A Novel Approach to Vehicle Validation using Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR)

Revanth Rampal, Dr. Co-Author, Prof. Supervisor

Published in: International Journal of Advanced Computer Science and Applications (IJACSA), Vol. 12, No. 5, 2021

Abstract

This paper presents a cutting-edge vehicle validation system utilizing Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR). By integrating advanced machine learning algorithms such as YOLO and leveraging OpenCV, our system achieves a significant improvement in recognition accuracy by 30% over traditional methods. We developed a user-friendly GUI with Tkinter for seamless user interaction.
